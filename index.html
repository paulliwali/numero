How to Play

Simply use the WASD Keys as Player 1 and Arrow Keys as Player 2 to control your respective dice.

The Goal of Numéro!

Numéro! is designed to provoke the player's ability in spatial judgment and teach the concept of pattern recognition and strategic planning. To learn more about spatial judgment and the nine modalities of intelligence, please refer to Howard Garnder's book Frames of Mind.
